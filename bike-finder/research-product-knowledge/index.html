Notes:

  • Important product factors of selecting bikes:
    • Location to ride
    • Maintainance
    • Use
    • Size
  • Buyer might consider how and where to ride the bike, and do they have any plan about the further maintainance once they purchase the bike. Also, they need to consider the size of their bikes, so it fits their height. Based on these information, they can begin to check different types of bikes.
  • Types of bikes:
    • Road bikes, hybrid bikes and electric bikes are suitable for paved roads and bike paths
    • Hybrid bikes and electric bikes best fit pavement (from pristine to potholed) and natural surfaces
    • Gravel and touring bikes suit all-road riding (from asphalt to outback)
    • Mountain bikes and electric bikes dirt roads and trails
  • With these information, users will quickly find which type of bike they want to purchase. Then they can locate specific bikes following the keywords on the shopping sites. Some websites such as REI provide keywords and filterings to help users define their desired bike types.
  • Accessories
    • Helmets
    • Park tools
    • Bells and lights
    • Cycling computer
  • Users might find these accessories useful.
  • Maintainance and Post-purchase Services:
    • Maintainance of a bike is a serious thing. You might need to buy some accessories. Sometimes you can get post-purchase services or maintainance from local retailers or companies.
