Feature Organization

Home Page

  • The Home page will be a large picture with either a bike or an illustration of bikes. Beside it, it will be the search bar and a navigation on top and a log in/sign up button.
  • The navigation will contain these features:
  • Categories
  • Brands
  • My cart
  • Viewing History
  • Some of these will have a dropdown menu.
  • Scrolling down the page we have thumbnails and all the categories of bikes. If the user click the “categories” above, they will be directed to this part of the page.

Search Page

  • Search page will have the same navigation of top and an extra “home” button.
  • A “sort by” button: price/sales/popular in your area/
  • Keywords of searching will be in a sidebar. User can filter them for a more precise search.
  • (also might add an “exclude” filter)
  • There will be thumbnails of products on the page, sort by the best sellers in default.

Recently Viewed Page

  • Has thumbnails of product prictures user recently viewed.
  • (has a "add to cart" button at each product?)
  • When the mouse is on the "recently viewed" button on home page, it will show the five most recent viewed products.

Product Page

  • Pictures of the product and the clear product name and description.
  • A dropdown for detailed product parameters.
  • Costumer reviews below, with the comments with pictures at top.
  • A "recommend for you" below the ratings.
  • "add to cart" or "buy now"
  • A feature to compare the product with others at same price level

My Cart

  • Users can check the items in the cart.
  • "add or drop" buttons beside each item
  • total price count
  • delivery options below, then a "check out" button
